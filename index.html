About

This is a summary of my career journey as a network engineer.

  • January - April 2020

    Network Technician Internship PT Nusantara Compnet Integrator

    This is the beginning of me starting to wander and work in the industrial world even though it's just an internship. This is a huge opportunity, In The Company I learned a lot working in a team, understand the basics of networking more depth, study the configuration and troubleshooting of Cisco devices, checking device to be sent to another perusaahan, and met with the client.

  • August - October 2020

    Mikrotik Network Olympiad 2020

    When I was in SMK, I participated in the Mikrotik Network Olympiad competition organized by APJII and Mikrotik. Because this competition is a team, I compete with Richi. Our competition started by writing a paper on how to implement CapsMAN on devices and adding a Wireless Mikrotik API Controller to manage CapSMan. Not only that, we still have to follow several other stages, namely making scientific paper videos, Written Tests, Lab Tests, and finally interviews. All of these processes taught us how to learn to be a Team. And from all these processes, we won 4th place at the national level in the Mikrotik Network Olympiad competition

  • October 2020 - April 2021

    Freelance mini ISP technician Sedjajar.net

    Although I was in school I was given the opportunity by my teacher as a freelance technician, a lot of knowledge I had acquired of managing the network so that a stable, mambuat topology, belajari fiber optic, involved the installation of the device in the client, and many more.

  • November - December 2020

    Aws cloud Educate web developer

    This competition is actually quite interesting for me because the time given is quite short, namely 10 days before the assessment, thank God, there are many free templates provided on google, but I still have a lot to change templates, what else will be shared with AWS. In those 10 days I learned a lot, namely changing templates, deploying to AWS, using GIT, and various other tools as support. Even though I didn't make it into the top 3, I made it into the top 10 and got Tupperware with the AWS slogan.

  • April 2021

    Competency Competition for Vocational High School Students at the East Java Work Area

    This competition is organized by the Ministry of Education and Culture. This competition taught me how to be a network and server infrastructure manager. For the case of a network not far from Mikrotik and Cisco, the case is only to connect our server to the committee server using a participating Mikrotik with VLAN, VPN, etc. methods. For server case, just basic web server, DNS server, HTTPS, etc. This competition requires speed, because there is a mistake in my browser so that other work be piled up and in the end I just got the 5th place.

  • April 2021 - Now

    Network Engineer at Privy ID

    This was the beginning of my career in the industry, although previously I've been familiar with the work that I have mentioned above. I got the opportunity to join this StartUp company, at the beginning of my entry I was given EXAM MTCRE, MTCTCE, and MTCUME, a challenge because the exam is only in a week and the exam date is only 1 day apart. Alhamdulillah, I finally got all these certifications. I work here under the guidance of Mikrotik's Temperature Master, Mr. Supono. I got a lot of new knowledge, and he taught me a lot of knowledge and mindset that I might not have gotten during school.
